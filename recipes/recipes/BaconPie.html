Bacon Pie Recipe

Pancetta, garlic, dijon mustard, white wine and tarragon - all come together to make something very special.

Ingridients

  • 1 quantity flaky pie dough, (see recipe below)
  • plain flour, for dusting
  • 1 egg yolk, whisked with 1 tbsp water

    • Fillings

  • 75g pancetta, diced
  • 500g cooked chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces (thighs or breasts)
  • 80g unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 large leeks, halved, washed and chopped
  • 3 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • tarragon, roughly chopped to make 2 tbsp
  • 250ml white wine
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 500ml chicken stock
